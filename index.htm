About

Gallery Zero is a social and spatial project within the academy that creates conditions for student-led initiatives to become visible, connected, and active.

It emerges from the observation that many forms of self-organisation already exist within the school, yet they lack space, presence, and continuity.

Rather than designing a fixed object, it proposes a framework - through sequences, rituals, and spatial elements - that enables students to gather, organise, and take ownership. Gallery Zero is not a finished space, but an evolving platform for agency.
